Checking a box does not commit you in any way, but indicates that you’d like to explore some creative possibilities with us.

5D Fundamentals: Experience & Drawing

Provided by: Jose Annunziato

This art course explores the language of interactive experience as a compelling medium to communicate meaning. Examines how variables within the environment can change how we inhabit an experience physically, conceptually, and emotionally. Studies historical and contemporary examples of art and design projects designed as exchanges or experiences. Incorporates drawing as a means to understand the present and project potential future experiences. Students could assess a user experience and produce design solutions.

Community Writing

Provided by: Mike Weintraub

This course offers students an opportunity to study and practice writing in community contexts through advocacy writing, service-learning, community research, and/or community publishing. Students will work on an advocacy writing project in collaboration with a partner which could include grant application support or other community events.
